Image to Text Guide

Overview

Extracting text from images is easy and secure with our free web app. No registration required.

How it works

Upload your image, and our OCR engine processes it in-memory. No images are stored.

Step-by-step

  1. Go to the Home page.
  2. Click the upload area and select your image.
  3. Wait for the text extraction to complete.
  4. Copy or download the extracted text.

Accuracy tips

  • Use high-resolution images.
  • Ensure text is clear and unobstructed.
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, etc.

Privacy note

We do not store your images. All processing is done in-memory.
